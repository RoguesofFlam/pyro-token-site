Introduction

Rogues of Flame is a competitive survival game set in a dark, elemental pixel-art universe. Built on Solana, it mixes fast-paced 1v1 arcade action with a crypto-powered ecosystem.

Matches are based on pure skill — real players or AI opponents with equal stats face off. The one who survives longer earns crypto rewards. This creates a sustainable GameFi loop: no Ponzi mechanics, just winners and losers.

Characters evolve by earning XP through gameplay, or instantly by spending $BLOOD. The economy supports both free-to-play users and token investors.

Gameplay

Rogues of Flame is built on a simple yet addictive loop: enter a match, survive waves of enemies, and outlast your rival. Every game pits you against either a real player or an AI of equivalent strength.

The combat system is inspired by Vampire Survivors but with 1v1 arcade intensity. Players control a unique creature — like Voltkit or Pyrowl — each with their own elemental theme and evolution stages.

  • Earn XP by playing and defeating enemies
  • Use $BLOOD to evolve instantly

Every match has a winner — the survivor — and a loser. That core mechanic drives the token flow and keeps the economy clean, competitive, and rewarding.
